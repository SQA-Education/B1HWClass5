Shopping at the Market

I love shopping at the local market. The prices are cheaper than at the supermarket, and the vegetables are fresher too.

Today I compared three vendors. The first vendor's tomatoes were bigger but more expensive. The second vendor had the cheapest prices. The third vendor had the best quality - they were the freshest tomatoes!

The market is better than the supermarket for fresh produce. The supermarket is more convenient, but the market is more interesting.
